*[Example Solution]{.underline}*

$E = \sum_{}^{}$$\ \frac{kq}{r^{2}}$ $\widehat{r}$

${|\ E}_{Net}\ | = 2 \cdot |E_{- 2q,\ x}\ |$

$= 2 \cdot$ $\frac{k(2q)}{r^{2}}$ $\cdot \cos 45{^\circ}$

$= 2 \cdot$ $\frac{k(2q)}{{(\frac{1}{2}\ s)}^{2}}$ $\cdot \frac{1}{}$

${|\ E}_{Net}\ | =$ $\frac{8}{}$ $\frac{kq}{s^{2}}$
