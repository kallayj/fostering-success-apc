*[Example Solution]{.underline}*

$\sum_{}^{}F = ma$

$F_{B} = m$$(\frac{v^{2}}{R})$

$evB =$$\ \frac{mv^{2}}{R}$

$eB =$$\ \frac{mv}{R}$

$v =$$\ \frac{eBR}{m}$

$D = vt$

$\frac{1}{2}(2\pi R) =$ $\ (\frac{eBR}{m})$$\ t$

$t =$ $\ \frac{m\pi}{eB}$
