$F\cos\theta = \mu F_{N}$

$F_{N} = F_{g} + F_{app,\ y}$

$F_{N} = Mg + F\sin\theta$

$F\cos\theta = \mu F_{N}$

$F\cos\theta = \mu$($Mg + F\sin\theta$)

$F\cos\theta - \mu F\sin\theta = \mu Mg$

$F(\cos\theta - \mu\sin\theta) = \mu Mg$

$F =$ $\frac{\mu Mg}{\cos\theta - \mu\sin\theta}$
