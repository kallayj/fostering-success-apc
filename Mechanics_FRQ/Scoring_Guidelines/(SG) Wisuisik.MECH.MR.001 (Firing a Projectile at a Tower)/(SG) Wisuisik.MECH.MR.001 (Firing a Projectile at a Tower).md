*[Example Solution]{.underline}*

$\Delta v = \int_{}^{}a(t)dt$

$= \int_{0}^{\infty}a_{0}e^{- Ct}\ dt$

$= a_{0}\int_{0}^{\infty}e^{- Ct}\ dt$

$= a_{0}$ $\frac{e^{- Ct}}{- C}$ ${|^{\infty}}_{0}$

$= a_{0}(0 -$ $\frac{1}{- C}$$)$

$\Delta v =$ $\frac{a_{0}}{C}$

$v_{f} - v_{0} =$ $\frac{a_{0}}{C}$

$v_{f} = v_{D} +$ $\frac{a_{0}}{C}$
